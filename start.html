About

The Media Lab is an interdisciplinary center within the Department of Design, IIT Guwahati for experiments with digital forms of design and learning.

The primary focus is on film, video, audio, new media, digital culture, and technology. The lab is a site of non-formal learning practices, where ‘research by practice’ is stimulated.

The purpose is to promote the creative use of new technologies by providing a collaborative environment for research and experimentation at the intersection of art, technology, and culture.
